## Game Definitions

### BB1 — Pillar Stand
- **Category:** Balance (BAL) | **Difficulty:** 1 | **Duration:** 30s | **Bilateral:** Yes (50/50)
- **Purpose:** Bilateral weight-bearing stability. Baseline balance test for ALL spine + lower limb conditions
- **Primary metric:** Breach Count (lower = better) | **Secondary:** Max Sway °
- **Score range:** 0-50 breaches | **Dummy score:** 65 (raw composite)
- **Body regions:** Back, Neck, Shoulder, Knee, Hip, Ankle
- **Contraindicated when:** Risk RED (all games blocked)

### BB2 — Twin Leg Stand
- **Category:** Balance (BAL) | **Difficulty:** 1 | **Duration:** 30s | **Bilateral:** No
- **Purpose:** Narrowed-base bilateral stability. Increased proprioceptive demand
- **Primary metric:** Breach Count | **Secondary:** Max Sway °
- **Score range:** 0-50 | **Dummy score:** 58
- **Body regions:** Back, Neck
- **Contraindicated when:** Risk RED or ORANGE

### BB3 — One Leg Stand
- **Category:** Balance (BAL) | **Difficulty:** 2 | **Duration:** 35s | **Bilateral:** No
- **Purpose:** Unilateral proprioception. Identifies side-specific deficits
- **Primary metric:** Breach Count | **Secondary:** Max Sway °
- **Score range:** 0-60 | **Dummy score:** 45 (left: 42, right: 48)
- **Body regions:** Knee, Hip, Ankle
- **Contraindicated when:** Risk RED, ORANGE, or acute ankle/knee injury

### BB4 — Blindfold on a Leg
- **Category:** Balance (BAL) | **Difficulty:** 3 | **Duration:** 35s | **Bilateral:** No
- **Purpose:** Vestibular-only balance (eyes closed). Severe proprioceptive loss detection
- **Primary metric:** Breach Count | **Secondary:** Max Sway °
- **Score range:** 0-60 | **Dummy score:** 52
- **Body regions:** Ankle (chronic instability, sinus tarsi)
- **Contraindicated when:** Risk RED, ORANGE, YELLOW, or any acute lower limb
